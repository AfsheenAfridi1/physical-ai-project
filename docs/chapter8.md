# Chapter 8 — Bipedal Locomotion & Humanoid Motion Control

Humanoid walking is one of the hardest problems in robotics.

## 8.1 Balance Models

Robots use:

Inverted Pendulum Model

Zero Moment Point (ZMP)

Center of Mass (CoM) control

## 8.2 Gait Generation

Walking cycle includes:

Stance phase

Swing phase

Foot placement

Dynamic balancing

## 8.3 Whole-Body Control

Coordinates 20–40 joints at once for:

Walking

Squatting

Arm movements

Balancing

## 8.4 Manipulation

Humanoid hands perform:

Grasping

Turning knobs

Picking objects

Precise alignment
